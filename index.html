About Us

We provide low-income individuals with meaningful access to legal representation, through the use of technology.

Clients

Find free legal help.

You need legal help, but can’t afford an attorney. Proboknow can help you by connecting you with a pro bono attorney – someone who is committed to helping low-income clients free-of-charge. Proboknow is free and easy to use – all you have to do is sign up and briefly describe your case.

Attorneys Seeking Practical Experience

Gain the experience you need.

You’re an attorney looking to gain practical experience – to either enhance your employability, start your own practice, transition into a new practice area, or just become a better lawyer. Proboknow will connect you with exactly the sorts of pro bono cases you’re interested in, along with a mentor, and a wealth of other resources to help you through the process. You get the experience you need through helping someone in need.

Experienced Attorneys

Make a difference.

You’re an experienced attorney interested in helping low-income clients. You can quickly and conveniently connect with low-income clients in your interest areas, and either assist them directly or make an even bigger difference by helping budding attorneys through their pro bono work as a mentor. Proboknow can connect you with exactly the sort of pro bono case you find compelling, as well as an attorney you’re interested in mentoring.

How It Works

  • Clients
  • Attorneys Seeking Practical Experience
  • Experienced Attorneys
1.
You need a lawyer in Orange County, CA, but can't afford one.
2.
Verify your income to qualify for free legal help. If you don’t qualify for free legal help, no worries – you can find affordable legal help on the Lowboknow marketplace.
3.
Briefly describe your legal claim, and it will be listed for the attorneys on Proboknow.
4.
When an attorney is interested in your case, he or she will contact you for a free legal consultation to better understand your case.
5.
If the attorney agrees to represent you, he or she will handle your case free-of-charge.
 
1.
You’re an Orange County, CA attorney who is looking to gain more practical experience – to enhance your employability, gain the expertise needed to hang your own shingle, or transition practice areas.
2.
Quickly verify your location, California Bar number, and that you have no disciplinary issues with the Bar, and create a profile.
3.
Search for a case that fits your interests and experiential goals. Once you find one, contact the potential client for an initial interview.
4.
Once you have found a case you would like to take on, search for and contact an experienced attorney to serve as your mentor on the case.
5.
Once the case is resolved, Proboknow will collect testimonials from your client and mentor, so that your good work is duly recognized, and so that you can bolster your resume.
 
1.
You’re an experienced Orange County, CA attorney who is looking to help a low-income person in need of legal representation through pro bono work.
2.
You can either assist a low-income client directly, or you can double the impact of your pro bono time by mentoring an attorney in need of experience through his/her pro bono work. Mentoring allows you to not only ensure quality representation to the client but also invaluable training to an attorney who needs it.
3.
After signing up for Proboknow, you’ll be connected with pro bono cases and new attorneys to mentor on these cases.
4.
Which attorneys you mentor, how you mentor, and on which cases you choose to mentor, are entirely up to you!
5.
Proboknow collects client testimonials, so that your good work is duly recognized.
